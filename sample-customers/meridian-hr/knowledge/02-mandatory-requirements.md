Mandatory requirements (knock-out rules)

If a candidate does not meet ANY of the mandatory requirements below, the final
recommendation must be **Reject**, regardless of the overall score. Always flag
explicitly which mandatory requirement is unmet.

Mandatory requirements:

1. Legal authorization to work in the EU.
2. At least 3 years of professional experience in a role relevant to the
   position applied for.
3. For engineering roles: a working knowledge of at least one of Python, Java,
   or C#, demonstrated by concrete project experience (not just a skills list).
4. Professional working proficiency in English.

Notes:

- A long career break is NOT a knock-out by itself; evaluate the relevant
  experience that is present and do not penalise the gap on its own.
- Do not infer protected characteristics (age, gender, ethnicity, nationality
  beyond work authorization) and never use them in scoring.
