Scoring thresholds and recommendation

Use the overall score (0–100) together with the mandatory requirements to reach
a final recommendation. These thresholds may be updated by HR over time.

Recommendation thresholds:

| Overall score | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| 80–100 | **Advance** — strong candidate, move to interview. |
| 70–79 | **Advance with reservations** — interview, probe the weak areas. |
| 55–69 | **Hold** — borderline; keep on file or consider for other roles. |
| 0–54 | **Reject** — does not meet the bar for this role. |

Overriding rule:

- Any unmet **mandatory requirement** forces a **Reject**, even if the overall
  score is high. State which requirement was not met.

Output expectations:

- Always show the per-criterion scores, the weighted overall score, the
  strengths, the gaps, and the final recommendation.
- Be transparent about which rule drove the decision and cite its source title.
