CV evaluation criteria and weighting

These are the CURRENT criteria the HR office uses to score every candidate CV.
They may be updated from time to time — always use the latest uploaded version.

Each candidate is scored from 0 to 100 as the weighted sum of the criteria
below. Score each criterion from 0 to 100, then apply its weight.

| Criterion | Weight | What we look for |
|---|---|---|
| Relevant experience | 35% | Years and depth of experience directly relevant to the target role. |
| Technical skills | 25% | Match between the candidate's skills and the role's required stack. |
| Education & certifications | 15% | Degree relevance and professional certifications. |
| Impact & achievements | 15% | Concrete, measurable results (metrics, ownership, delivery). |
| Communication & clarity | 10% | A clear, well-structured CV with no major gaps left unexplained. |

Overall score = sum(criterion_score × weight).
