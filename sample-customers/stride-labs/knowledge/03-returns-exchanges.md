Stride Labs — Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Test Run
- Every pair comes with a 30-day Test Run guarantee. Take them on real runs,
  not just around the house.
- If they are not right, return them within 30 days of delivery for a full
  refund or a free size/model exchange — even if they have been worn outdoors.

How to start a return or exchange
- Go to the Orders section of your Stride Labs account and select "Return or
  exchange", or contact Stride Labs Support with your order number.
- You will receive a prepaid shipping label by email. Returns are free within
  the country of purchase.
- Pack the shoes in the original box where possible. Refunds are issued to the
  original payment method within 5–7 business days of the warehouse receiving
  the pair.

Exchanges
- Size or model exchanges ship as soon as the return is scanned by the carrier,
  so you are not left waiting.
- If the new model costs more, you pay the difference; if it costs less, the
  difference is refunded.

Defective pairs (separate from the Test Run)
- Manufacturing defects are covered for 12 months from purchase (see the
  General FAQ for warranty details). Defective pairs are replaced free and do
  not count against the 30-day window.

What is NOT eligible
- Pairs returned after 30 days that are not defective.
- Shoes damaged by misuse, alteration, or non-running use.
- Final-sale or clearance items, which are marked as such at checkout.
