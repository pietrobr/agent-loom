Stride Labs — General FAQ

Which shoe should I choose for marathon training?
- Train in the Stride Tempo for the bulk of your easy and long runs, use the
  Pulse for tempo and speed sessions, and race in the Aero on marathon day.
- Many runners "rotate" all three to spread the load and stay fresh.

Shipping
- Standard shipping is free on all orders. Orders ship within 1 business day and
  arrive in 2–5 business days depending on your location.
- Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available at checkout for a flat fee.
- Stride Labs ships across the EU, UK, US and Canada. Duties and taxes are shown
  at checkout for international orders.

Order tracking
- A tracking link is emailed when your order ships. You can also see live status
  in the Orders section of your Stride Labs account.

Warranty
- All shoes are warranted against manufacturing defects for 12 months from the
  date of purchase. Defects include sole separation, seam failure and upper
  tears under normal running use.
- Warranty claims are replaced free of charge. Normal wear, such as worn-out
  cushioning or outsole, is not a defect (see Shoe Care & Durability).

Payment & gift cards
- We accept major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
- Stride Labs gift cards never expire and can be combined with one promo code
  per order.

Talk to a human
- For anything not covered here — bulk/team orders, sponsorship, or a tricky
  sizing or warranty case — ask to be connected with the Stride Labs Support
  team and we will follow up by email.
