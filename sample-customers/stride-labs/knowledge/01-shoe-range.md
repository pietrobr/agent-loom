Stride Labs — Shoe Range

Stride Labs designs performance running shoes engineered and tested at the
Stride Lab in Boulder, Colorado. The current range covers four use cases, from
daily training to race day.

1) Stride Tempo — Daily Trainer
   - Balanced 10 mm drop, responsive EVA-blend midsole
   - Durable rubber outsole built for high weekly mileage
   - Best for: everyday training, easy runs, beginners building a base

2) Stride Pulse — Tempo & Speedwork
   - Lightweight 8 mm drop with a snappy, energetic foam
   - Breathable engineered-mesh upper, secure midfoot lockdown
   - Best for: tempo runs, intervals, 5K–10K race day

3) Stride Aero — Carbon Marathon Racer
   - Full-length carbon plate over a super-critical foam midsole
   - 6 mm drop, ultra-light at 215 g (US M9)
   - Best for: half-marathon and marathon racing, peak-day efforts

4) Stride Terra — Trail Runner
   - Aggressive 4 mm lugs, rock plate underfoot, 6 mm drop
   - Water-resistant upper with a reinforced toe cap
   - Best for: technical trails, mud, gravel and mountain routes

All models are unisex in fit and offered in standard and wide widths. Colours
rotate seasonally; the core "Lab Black" and "Signal Orange" colourways are
always available.
