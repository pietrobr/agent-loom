Aurelia Motors — Warranty & Coverage

New Vehicle Warranty
- Every new Aurelia is covered by a 4-year / unlimited-mileage warranty.
- Coverage starts on the date of delivery to the first owner.
- The warranty is fully transferable to subsequent owners at no cost, which
  helps preserve resale value.

Battery Warranty (Aurelia Eterna)
- The high-voltage battery is covered for 8 years or 160,000 km, whichever
  comes first.
- Aurelia guarantees the battery will retain at least 70% of its original
  usable capacity during the warranty period; otherwise it is repaired or
  replaced at no charge.

Paint & Corrosion
- Bespoke paint finishes are warranted for 4 years against defects.
- The body structure is warranted against perforation from corrosion for
  12 years.

What is NOT covered
- Normal wear items: tyres, brake pads and discs, wiper blades, and clutch
  friction surfaces.
- Damage from accidents, misuse, track use, or unauthorised modifications.
- Maintenance performed outside the Aurelia Authorised Service network.

Extended Care
- Owners may purchase "Aurelia Extended Care" to add up to 4 additional years
  of coverage. It must be activated before the original warranty expires.
- Extended Care includes 24/7 roadside assistance and concierge transport.
