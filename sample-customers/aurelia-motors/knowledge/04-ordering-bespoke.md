Aurelia Motors — Ordering, Bespoke & Delivery

Placing an order
- Orders are placed through an Aurelia Atelier (showroom) or with an Aurelia
  Client Advisor.
- A refundable commissioning deposit of 10% secures a build slot.
- Typical build time from order confirmation to delivery is 4 to 6 months,
  depending on bespoke content.

Aurelia Bespoke program
- Choose from over 200 exterior paint colours, or commission a fully custom
  colour matched to any sample you provide.
- Interior options include hand-stitched leather, sustainable wool, real wood
  and carbon-fibre veneers, and personalised embroidery.
- Owners may add a unique commissioning plaque with their name or a personal
  inscription.

Delivery experience
- Standard delivery takes place at your nearest Aurelia Atelier with a personal
  handover and feature walkthrough.
- "Atelier Collection" allows owners to collect the car at the Modena factory,
  including a tour and lunch with the build team.
- Home delivery anywhere in the country is available on request.

Changing or cancelling an order
- Specification changes are possible until the car enters the paint stage.
- The commissioning deposit is fully refundable if cancelled before production
  begins; once production starts it becomes non-refundable.
