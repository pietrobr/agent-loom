Aurelia Motors — Model Range

Aurelia Motors hand-builds four luxury models. Every car is assembled at the
Aurelia Atelier in Modena and personalised through the Aurelia Bespoke program.

1) Aurelia Stellare — Grand Tourer
   - 4.0L twin-turbo V8, 612 hp, 0-100 km/h in 3.4 s
   - 2+2 seating, full-grain Tuscan leather as standard
   - Ideal for long-distance touring in absolute comfort

2) Aurelia Vento — Roadster
   - 3.8L twin-turbo V8, 580 hp, retractable hardtop in 14 seconds
   - Two seats, lightweight carbon-fibre tub
   - The most driver-focused car in the range

3) Aurelia Soluna — Luxury SUV
   - 4.0L V8 mild-hybrid, 550 hp, adaptive air suspension
   - Five seats, optional rear "Executive Lounge" package
   - All-wheel drive with terrain modes

4) Aurelia Eterna — Electric Flagship Saloon
   - Dual-motor all-electric, 700 hp, 620 km WLTP range
   - 350 kW fast charging: 10-80% in about 22 minutes
   - Four seats with reclining rear thrones and a glass roof

All models are limited production. Annual output is capped at 1,800 cars
worldwide to preserve exclusivity.
