Aurelia Motors — Ownership Services & FAQ

Aurelia Privilege membership
- Every owner automatically receives Aurelia Privilege membership for the life
  of their ownership.
- Benefits include 24/7 concierge, priority service booking, invitations to
  Aurelia driving events, and access to the owners' lounge at the Modena
  factory.

Roadside assistance
- Complimentary 24/7 roadside assistance is included for the duration of the
  warranty across the country and neighbouring regions.
- If your car cannot be repaired the same day, Aurelia arranges alternative
  luxury transport so your plans are not interrupted.

Charging the Aurelia Eterna
- The Eterna supports 350 kW DC fast charging: roughly 10-80% in about 22
  minutes under ideal conditions.
- A home wall-box charger is included with every Eterna; professional
  installation can be arranged by Aurelia.
- The car provides access to a network of premium public charging partners
  through the Aurelia Owner app.

Test drives
- Private test drives are arranged by appointment at an Aurelia Atelier.
- For serious commissions, a multi-day "Extended Experience" drive can be
  arranged with a concierge.

Frequently asked questions
- Q: Can I track my car's build progress?
  A: Yes. The Aurelia Owner app shows live build milestones and photos from the
     Atelier.
- Q: Are Aurelia cars available outside Europe?
  A: Aurelia delivers worldwide through its Atelier partners; lead times vary by
     region.
- Q: Do you offer financing?
  A: Tailored financing and leasing are available through Aurelia Financial
     Services; speak with your Client Advisor for options.

For anything not covered here, an Aurelia Client Advisor is available 24/7
through the concierge line.
