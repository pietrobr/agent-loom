Aurelia Motors — Servicing & Maintenance

Service intervals
- Petrol models (Stellare, Vento, Soluna): every 12 months or 15,000 km,
  whichever comes first.
- Electric flagship (Eterna): every 24 months or 30,000 km, whichever comes
  first. Electric models require less routine maintenance.

How to book a service
- Book through the Aurelia Owner app, the owner web portal, or by contacting
  your dedicated Aurelia Client Advisor.
- Aurelia offers complimentary collection and delivery of your car within
  100 km of any Authorised Service Centre.
- During service, owners are offered a courtesy Aurelia vehicle on request.

Aurelia Care Plan
- A prepaid maintenance plan covering all scheduled servicing for 5 years or
  75,000 km.
- It is transferable with the car and can be purchased at any time within the
  first 12 months of ownership.

Tyres and consumables
- Aurelia recommends rotating tyres every 10,000 km.
- Only Aurelia-approved tyre specifications should be fitted to preserve the
  handling balance and the warranty.

Software updates (Eterna)
- The Eterna receives over-the-air software updates at no charge.
- Major feature updates are released approximately twice per year.
